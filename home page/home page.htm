LEARNING ABOUT COLLABORATIVE TELEPRESENCE






The word telepresence can be defined as, according to collins dictionary, the use of virtual reality technology to operate machinery by remote control or to create the effect of being at a different or imaginary location and Collaborative is know as a piece of work that is done by two or more people or groups working together.

WHAT IS COLLABORATIVE TELEPRESENCE YOU MAY ASK?


Collaborative telepresence is the technology which allows the human sense to feel as if they were present or to give the appearance of being present at a place other than their true location with more than one person achieving a common goal.

Collaborative telepresence is one that has many impacts on things that are apart of our daily life. These are:

  • Education

  • Media and Entertainment

  • Social Media

  • Media and Entertainment

  • Health Care
